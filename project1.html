Cristiano Ronaldo

One of the greatest football players of all time

Here's a time line of Cristiano's life:

  • August 12, 2003 - Transfer to Manchester United
  • August 16, 2003 -Premier League debut
  • August 20, 2003 -International debut
  • September 9, 2003 -UEFA Champions League debut
  • November 1, 2003 -First Premier League goal
  • September 26, 2006 -First UEFA Champions League goal
  • May 22, 2004 -First trophy (FA Cup)
  • June 11, 2006 -First World Cup appearance
  • June 17, 2006 -First World Cup goal
  • January 12, 2008 -First hat-trick (Against Newcastle United)
  • December 02, 2008 -First Ballon d'Or win
  • June 11, 2009 -Transfer to Real Madrid
  • August 29, 2009 -Real Madrid debut (Against Deportivo La Coruna)
  • August 29, 2009 -First goal for Real Madrid
  • October 17, 2015 -All-time Real Madrid top-scorer
  • July 10, 2016 -Euro Cup win
  • December 7, 2017 -Fifth Ballon d'Or
  • May 26, 2018 -Fifth Champions League
  • June 15, 2018 -First World Cup hat-trick (Against Spain)
  • August 18, 2018 -Serie A debut for Juventus
  • September 16, 2018 -Serie A first goal
  • May 12, 2021 -100th Juventus goal (Against Sassuolo)
  • May 23, 2021 -Serie A Golden Boot
  • August 27, 2021 -Return to Manchester United
  • September 1, 2021 -All-time international top-scorer
  • December 2, 2021 -800th Career goal
  • April 16, 2022 -50th club hat-trick (Against Norwich City)
  • April 23, 2022 -100th Premier League goal
  • January 1, 2023 -Transfer to Al Nassr
  • February 9, 2023 -First Al Nassr hat-trick

If you have time, you should read more about this incredible life on his Wikipedia entry.
